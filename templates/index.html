Corbett. / (ˈkɔːbət) / noun. Mountaineering any separate mountain peak between 762m and 914m high: originally used of Scotland only, but now sometimes extended to other parts of the British Isles. Slang.


The less well known siblings of the Munros, at current reckoning there are 222 to bag, and so far 678 people have climbed them all.
